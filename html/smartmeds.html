PROBLEM STATEMENT

Medical adherence is far from ideal worldwide. According to the WHO, adherence among chronic disease patients in developed countries averages only 50%. Only 80% of new prescriptions are filled in the United States each year, and only 50% of those filled were taken correctly in terms of "timing, dosage, frequency, and duration" (Neiman, 2017).
Our project seeks for a solution: How can we help patients and caregivers better manage medication and side effects during recovery?

DESIGN OVERVIEW

The target users of SmartMEDs are patients and caregivers of patients who need to take medicines regularly. Users can use the product daily for documentation and follow the doctor's instructions. Considering the demographic of our target users consists of a great number of elderly people who might not be confident with technology, to improve the product's usability and enhance user experience, SmartMEDs designs a physical system: a Bluetooth Pillbox to work alongside the digital mobile application.
